# WAKE
### The Worksite Approval Knowledge Engine
*Every permit, decoded.*

---

**What it is, in one line:**
WAKE is a tool that takes the guesswork, delays, and headaches out of pulling construction permits.

---

**The problem it solves:**
Every contractor knows the nightmare. You land a job, you're ready to work — then the permit stops you cold. Different rules for the county, the state, sometimes the feds. Forms that don't explain themselves. A reviewer kicks it back over one detail and you lose weeks. Some guys pay expediters thousands per project just to deal with it. Everyone else grinds through it the hard way. It's one of the biggest hidden costs in the whole business, and nobody ever got trained on it.

---

**What WAKE does:**
You tell it the basics of your job — where it is, what you're building, the size, anything unusual about the site. In seconds it hands you:

- Every agency and permit you'll have to deal with
- The local rules that get permits rejected in that specific county
- The exact reasons jobs like yours get kicked back — so you fix it before you submit
- A realistic timeline, so you can tell the customer the truth instead of guessing
- A written permit description you can copy straight onto the application

What used to take days of phone calls and waiting now takes about a minute.

---

**Why it's different from other software:**
WAKE gets smarter every time anyone uses it. When a contractor runs a job and reports back what happened — approved, or kicked back for X — the tool learns it. So the next contractor doing a similar job in that county gets an even sharper answer. Picture every builder in an area quietly feeding one shared brain that everybody gets to use. Nobody can copy that, because they don't have the network feeding it. That's what makes it a real company, not just an app.

---

**Where it starts, where it goes:**
It's launching in the marine/dock world first — that's the beachhead. But every trade that pulls a permit has the exact same headache: pools, solar, roofing, decks, additions. The tool doesn't care what the trade is. Start with one, own it, then roll it out trade by trade.

---

**Current status (straight version):**
Right now WAKE is a working proof of concept — you can watch it run a real job and see exactly where it's headed. The full engine (live data connections and the learning network) is what gets built next. It's early, which is exactly why it's the right time to get involved.
