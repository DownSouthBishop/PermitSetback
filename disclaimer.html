Disclaimer

Setback is an informational tool, not a legal or engineering service. It does not replace an official determination from the permitting agencies that actually govern your project, and it is not legal advice.

What this means in practice

  • Agency names, requirements, and rules described in a roadmap are drawn from public information and, over time, real reported outcomes — not a live connection to any government system.
  • Where Setback isn't certain a specific local rule applies, it says so in general terms and flags it for confirmation, rather than inventing a precise number or citation.
  • A draft permit narrative is meant to be reviewed and adapted, not submitted verbatim without reading it first.
  • Requirements change, and enforcement varies between jurisdictions and even between reviewers in the same office. Always confirm current requirements directly with the issuing agencies before submitting an application or beginning construction.

Geography

Data quality is strongest for U.S. jurisdictions. Output for locations outside the U.S. should be treated as unreliable.
