Questions worth asking before you pay us anything

Is this legal advice?

No. Setback is a research shortcut, not a legal determination. It's meant to be reviewed with your engineer and the permitting authorities — always confirm current requirements with the issuing agencies before you submit.

Do you guarantee my permit gets approved?

No — we guarantee the packet, not the county's decision. If you submit the application as our packet drafted it and the county rejects it, you get your money back, in full. You keep the packet. The guarantee covers rejection of the application as drafted; it doesn't cover approval timelines, inspection outcomes, or scope you changed after drafting. Counties have the final word on requirements — always confirm before you file.

What if Setback misses something?

If we miss an agency or permit your project clearly needed, that pull is refunded. Report a rejected outcome on your project page and file a claim right there, or email support@setback.example with the rejection notice or the jurisdiction's own code section, within 30 days — that's the whole process.

Do I need an account?

No account to generate a roadmap. If you want to save it and come back later, we'll send a link to your email — no password, ever.

What trades and locations do you cover?

Any construction or improvement trade, any US location — pools, decks, roofing, solar, fencing, additions, garages, and beyond.

Why is this so much cheaper than an expediter?

Because you're not paying for someone's time on hold with the county. You're paying for the lookup itself — the part that actually takes 60 seconds once you know where to look.
